I am Wesley-M, the developer behind Ssnake, a game based on the classical one, but with a new face! I hope that the game will remind you of the fun of snake!

Here is the rules:

  • Don't clash with your body (LoL)

As you will notice the balls have different colors, and each color has an effect on your snake.

The possible effects are:

  • Game over instantenously
  • Key to winning
  • Increase ratio of vision field
  • Decrease ratio of vision field
  • Plus three points
  • Minus three points
  • Increase speed
  • Decrease speed

Discover the colors and its effects ^-^
